# TEKNOFEST 2026: Visual Identity Style Guide

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Electric Blue:** `#00D2FF` (Primary - Innovation)
- **Cyber Purple:** `#9D50BB` (Secondary - Future)
- **Neon Green:** `#39FF14` (Highlight - Energy)
- **Deep Space:** `#010409` (Background - Tech)

## 🔠 Typography
- **Headlines:** `Orbitron` or `Rajdhani` (Cyberpunk aesthetic)
- **Body Text:** `Inter` or `Roboto` (Clean readability)

## 📐 Design Principles
- **Futuromodernism:** Clean lines with jagged accents.
- **Glassmorphism:** Using translucent layers for digital interfaces.
- **Dynamic Energy:** Use of gradients and glow effects to simulate high-tech activity.
